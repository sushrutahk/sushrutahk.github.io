    OUR FACILITY

    Our facility includes 5 acres farm and a herb of cows. The cows are fed with high quality food , good shed and we also ensure that dairy cows are comfortable and managed in an environment to improve their health and prevent diseases.

    OUR PRODUCTS

    Our products include milk, ghee, cheese, butter, fresh and paneer. Our milk is instantly pasteurized ,chilled and packed in bottles . Milk being our main product is delivered fresh and chilled to 4 degree Celsius to your doorstep.

    WHO WE ARE

    We started in 2013 as a small unit and now its well-established. In the beginning we had very few cows and were supplying to the nearby areas, as the demand for our milk was high we started rearing ample of cows.

    Welcome to Vintage Pasture, where we nurture our passion for producing fresh, delicious, and wholesome dairy products that we love to share with you. Our milk comes straight from the family farm, delivered fresh to your doorstep in a few hours, unlike most others, and is available in four delicious variants.
    Every drop of milk comes from a single source: the Vintage Pasture. And we only make as much as our cows can produce. Our team spares no effort to ensure our prized Holstein cows are comfortable and happy, as it guarantees the best quality milk. And since we want you to taste its natural goodness, we don’t add any preservatives. The result is pure, wholesome milk that reaches your doorstep within hours of milking,perfectly chilled for zero degradation.
